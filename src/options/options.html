Tab Jump List

Thank you for using Tab Jump List!

To view your recent tabs, click on the extension icon in the toolbar.
Note: The extension only stores the tabs that were opened after the installation.

Shortcuts

  • Go to previous tab: Ctrl + O
  • Go to next tab: Ctrl + I
  • Open the recent tabs list: Ctrl + M

Settings

To change the keyboard shortcuts for the extension:

  • Go to chrome://extensions/shortcuts
  • Find the section Tab Jump List
  • Assign the keys of your choice
